# ShiftSync Commercial License

This document summarizes commercial usage terms for ShiftSync.

## When a commercial license is required

A paid commercial license is required for any business/commercial use, including:

- self-hosting ShiftSync for internal business operations;
- providing ShiftSync to clients, franchises, or affiliated entities;
- resale, white-label, managed hosting, or embedded product use;
- paid implementation or support services where ShiftSync is a core deliverable.

## Commercial options

### 1) Hosted SaaS subscription (recommended)

- Starter: $79/location/month
- Growth: $149/location/month
- Multi-site: $299+/month (custom)

### 2) Commercial self-host license

Available under separate paid terms, including:

- commercial-use grant;
- update/support scope;
- deployment and user/location limits;
- billing, renewal, and termination terms.
